Press Service - $1,800 USD

We craft tailored press campaigns to elevate the visibility of artists and labels within electronic music media and beyond. With a deep understanding of the electronic music cultural landscape, we know which tastemakers, mediums, and platforms will amplify your music in a genuine way that resonates with your target audience.

DSP Promo Service - $750 USD

We pitch your release directly to our editorial contacts in the dance/electronic departments across Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, Tidal, Deezer, and Soundcloud, significantly enhancing the standard backend pitching by distro, label, or management.

Our DSP service also includes a bespoke third-party playlist placement and algorithmic playlist optimization campaign. We ask you for a list of ‘reference tracks’ from similar sized artists in your track’s micro niche, and pitch to curators who have placed these and similar tracks in their playlist. This targeted approach yields ‘high-quality listens’, tailoring your release’s algorithmic associations to improve subsequent algorithmic playlist placements.

Club/Tastemaker DJ Promo Service - $750 USD

Our DJ promo service ensures your release reaches key global tastemaker DJs, helping your music gain traction at festivals, clubs, and beyond. With strong relationships across the electronic music spectrum, we strategically place your track into the hands - or rather onto the USBs - of influential DJs, generating organic buzz via dancefloors across the world.

Radio Promo Service - $500 USD

We pitch your track for airplay across our network of international radio DJs and music programmers, spanning terrestrial, internet, and satellite stations.

Additionally, we offer guest mix seeding, pitching stations on an exclusive guest mix (to be recorded after the station confirms interest), and/or syndicating one non-exclusive guest mix to stations and shows with recurring guest mix slots.
